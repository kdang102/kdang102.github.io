Initial Design

These were the initial designs of the "quests" section of our prototype.

The initial design overall had a good strcuture, but the colors were inconsistent and blended into the background.

The color of the text made it difficult to read. This task card was a good start, but needed more options for repitition.

The image was a bit boring, and the rewards were a bit confusing. It is unclear what the points, exp, and prize are.


Heuristic Evaluations, Feedback, and Iteration

After receiving peer evaluations based on usability heuristics, we made some changes to the design

The original design needed more contrast in colors for the user to clearly see what was selected at all times. We also added a tutorial button for new users.

This screen was changed for simular reasons. The was chosen to be a color with higher contrast to increase readibility. A repeat option was also added for convenience.

We received feedback on the original page being a bit too vague. Users often did nothave a good sense of their progres, so we removed extra information and added a progrss bar.


As mentioned before, a tutorial was added into the design to teach new users how to use the app. Each page even highlights the feature it is referring to.
